Goals

In 10 years, I plan to have my own startup. My passion is in education. Websites like Coursera and Udemy are changing the way colleges distribute knowledge, but I feel that the quality of textbooks is lacking. I have been a tutor for 2+ years, led recitations for CS111 at Rutgers for 2 semesters, and have taught students from middle school to Master's level in math and CS.

I found that all my students were very bright, but had difficulty because topics weren't presented accessibly. Towards that end, I wrote my own 150-page Calculus I & II textbook for tutoring. I've also built LearnMeSomething and NowTutor.Me — both aimed at democratizing education.
